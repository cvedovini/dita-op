prodinfo

The <prodinfo> metadata element in the prolog contains information about the product or products that are the subject matter of the current topic. The prodinfo element may be used to provide a more detailed definition of values used throughout the map or topic on the product attribute.

Example

<prolog>
 <metadata>
  <prodinfo>
   <prodname>Transcription Assistant</prodname>
    <vrmlist><vrm version="1" release="3" modification="1"/></vrmlist>
   <platform>Linux</platform>
  </prodinfo>
 </metadata>
</prolog>
